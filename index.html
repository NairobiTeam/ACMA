How did Coffee Puppies appear?

We are four teens who have set up this type of cafe, and, given our passion for reading,
we thought to combine these two wonderful things: coffee and books.
We wanted this place to make your life more beautiful, more creative and more caffeinated :).

With our partners, our coffee and our customers, we live in the following ways:

  • Creating a culture of hospitality and belonging, where everyone feels welcome.
  • Courage, questioning the status quo and finding new ways to develop the company and people.
  • Promptness, transparency, dignity and respect in relationships.
  • Maximum professionalism in everything we do, assuming responsibility for results.

We are motivated by performance through humanity. With regard to the reading list, the books you can find in our café are new in all areas: from fiction to personal development.
So what are you waiting for?
